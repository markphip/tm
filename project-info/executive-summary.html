November 13, 2006: RSE 1.0 has been released. Extensive Release Testing including the user community was extremely successful, bringing lots of bugs to light that could be fixed for the 1.0 release. There have been over 220 downloads with RC1 already, and we expect continued growth.

Next goals are an RSE 1.0.1 service release on December 15, 2006 and joining the Europa train with RSE 2.0M4 on Jan 4, 2007.
