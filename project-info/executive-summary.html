April 19, 2007: TM 2.0M6a has been released, providing good EFS support and thus finishing another plan item. Download Statistics show that RSE 1.0.1 downloads are at 2300 while recent 2.0 milestone downloads are at about 570, mostly through the Europa Discovery site. The TM Webinar on April 12 had 42 listeners peak attendance, and feedback was very positive. Mailing list and newsgroup activity is also growing, and we are frequently getting community contributions of code (2 larger ones are currently pending Legal Review on bugzilla). So over all the communities are growing very well.

For the upcoming 2.0M7 milestone, we still have some refactorings in the queue, but over all we are on track with our plan, and especially the pending improvements for the persistence providers look very promising.
